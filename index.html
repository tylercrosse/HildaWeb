About Me

My name is Hilda and I smell! After growing up in Las Vegas, Hilda LeStrange relocated to Richmond, VA, where she now happily resides. She attended Virginia Commonwealth University, where she received a Bachelor's Degree of Fine Art in Communication Arts, focusing mainly on graphic design and illustration. When she isn't working, she spends her free time doing other things pleasurable to the right-side brain, including photography, cartoon-watching, and house plant collecting. Contrary to popular belief, she is not related to Bellatrix Lestrange.


Her illustration entitled "Intoxication" was recently published in CMYK Magazine #55 under Top 100 New Creatives, released this Spring!
